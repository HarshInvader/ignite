If you are someone who isn’t aware of what cryptography is about, don’t be disheartened. We got you! Cryptography basically means to convert ordinary plain text into unintelligible text and vice-versa.

The concept of Cryptography in India is almost non-existent. Hence, to evoke interest in the same, we plan to introduce and explain what cryptography is through a game that also banks on your knowledge of Bollywood Trivia.

No, absolutely not! The event is designed in such a way that no prerequisite knowledge about any programming language is needed. All the information that you will need to WIN the competition will be provided to you. Happy ciphering-deciphering!

This event will help you understand the logic behind cryptography. What makes it unique is that, rather than using bookish examples that need to be ciphered, we’ll be using Bollywood dialogues which makes it more interesting, doesn’t it?

The game uses Caesar cipher, Vigenere cipher and Transposition Cipher. Links for their decoders: Caesar cipher: https://www.dcode.fr/caesar-cipher Vigenere cipher: https://www.dcode.fr/vigenere-cipher Transposition Cipher.:https://www.dcode.fr/transposition-cipher
